Expert CRM Reviews

HubSpot CRM

9.1/10

HubSpot CRM stands out as the most comprehensive free CRM available, making it perfect for startups and growing businesses.

Pros

  • Completely free forever plan
  • Intuitive, user-friendly interface
  • Excellent marketing integration
  • Strong reporting capabilities
  • Great mobile app

Cons

  • Advanced features are expensive
  • Limited customization in free plan
  • Can become pricey as you scale
Best For: Small to medium businesses looking for an all-in-one sales and marketing platform with strong free features.

Salesforce

8.8/10

The gold standard for enterprise CRM, Salesforce offers unmatched customization and scalability for large organizations.

Pros

  • Industry-leading features
  • Extensive customization
  • Massive app marketplace
  • Excellent scalability
  • Strong automation tools

Cons

  • Steep learning curve
  • Expensive pricing
  • Can be overwhelming for small teams
Best For: Large enterprises with complex sales processes that need extensive customization and have dedicated admin resources.

Pipedrive

9.0/10

Built by salespeople for salespeople, Pipedrive excels at visual sales pipeline management with exceptional ease of use.

Pros

  • Extremely intuitive interface
  • Excellent visual pipeline
  • Great mobile experience
  • Strong sales focus
  • Affordable pricing

Cons

  • Limited marketing features
  • Basic reporting in lower tiers
  • Fewer third-party integrations
Best For: Sales-focused teams that prioritize simplicity and want a CRM that's easy to adopt and use daily.

Zoho CRM

8.5/10

Part of the comprehensive Zoho suite, this CRM offers excellent value with strong automation and customization capabilities.

Pros

  • Great value for money
  • Strong automation features
  • Integrates with Zoho suite
  • Good customization options
  • Multiple deployment options

Cons

  • Interface can feel dated
  • Steeper learning curve
  • Limited third-party integrations
Best For: Small to medium businesses already using Zoho products or those needing advanced features at budget-friendly prices.

Freshworks CRM

8.7/10

Modern, AI-powered CRM that combines ease of use with intelligent features to help sales teams work more efficiently.

Pros

  • Modern, clean interface
  • Built-in phone and email
  • AI-powered insights
  • Good free plan
  • Excellent customer support

Cons

  • Limited advanced customization
  • Fewer third-party integrations
  • Still evolving feature set
Best For: Growing teams that want modern CRM features with AI capabilities and prefer an all-in-one solution.

Close CRM

8.9/10

Built specifically for inside sales teams, Close excels at phone-based selling with integrated calling and email capabilities.

Pros

  • Excellent built-in phone system
  • Great for inside sales teams
  • Strong email integration
  • Good mobile app
  • Solid reporting features

Cons

  • Higher price point
  • Limited marketing features
  • Fewer customization options
Best For: Inside sales teams and businesses that rely heavily on phone-based selling and need integrated calling features.
